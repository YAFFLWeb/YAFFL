YAFFL is the area's top adult flag football league with nearly a century of seasons played

From the battles of Harrisburg City Island (1980's), Community Center league (1990's), Red Lion Recreation (2000's), Springettsbury Township Park (2010's), to the current times and locations in York and Hanover, PA; our members have enjoyed weekends filled with hard work, athleticism, and creating bonds with others while holding one common goal: #justwin!
